Where It Started

Lewser is a family-run business, created by Sarah, Serris, and Sean Lew. Derived from their last name and the word “loser”, Lewser became a playful nickname for the Lew siblings. What started out as a joke soon sparked inspiration for a brand. individuality, the Lew siblings want you to feel comfortable and proud to wear the name of their brand, Lewser. As a family-oriented corporation, Lewser is built by the bond that brings these three together. Lewser is both a creative outlet for them to express their artistic ideas and a way for them to come together as one. Fueled by the love and support they have for one another, Lewser emphasizes the importance of family. They not only support one another but also aspire to push and inspire each other through their individual accomplishments. Today their line is limited to online shopping only, but plans to expand in the future to make their products more accessible to their customers.

Ordering and Payment

After adding your requested item to your bag, you can review, edit and/or delete items in your bag before checkout. Upon checkout, you must enter your shipping address (Note: Please carefully review all shipping information to ensure there are no errors). After placing your order, a confirmation page and number will be given to you. Order details will be sent to you by e-mail. Products will be sold to whoever completes checkout first. Putting a product in a cart does not reserve that product until it is checked out. Products will remain in consumer’s cart indefinitely, even when customer leaves site and comes back later. Cart will remain unchanged unless product went out of stock, only then will it be removed from their cart.

Returns and Exchanges

Returns and exchanges must be made within 30 days of the delivery date (the day you receive your order). All items must be unworn and unwashed, attached with all original tags. Please note that this does not apply for international orders. Exchanges are limited to defective products (defects not caused by improper use or handling) and changes in size only. After 30 days, we will not accept any returns or exchanges from domestic (within the US) orders. For returns, we will credit your original form of payment. You will receive an email notifying you of the refund. If you do not receive an email from us, please notify us at Contact Us. As shipping charges are non-refundable, you will be responsible for handling return/exchange shipping fees as well. Look below for instructions on how to return or exchange your item.

Shipping Information

Lewser uses USPS for all shipments, including international. The shipping rates are dependent on the location, size, weight, and quantity of the order. Look below for more details on the shipping fees. The shipping fee will be automatically calculated and included when you checkout your product(s). How to track your order: Once your order has been fulfilled, you will receive an email confirming your product’s shipment. The email will include a USPS tracking number and a link to view the status of your order. Please inform us at Contact Us, if you do not receive a tracking code. NOTE: Once your item has been shipped, USPS is responsible for the location of your package; you can use your tracking number to find your package. However, if you think your package is lost or delayed, contact USPS for more details. Please refer to their Missing Mail & Lost Packages page for the steps on how to find your package.
